What is @planettvguide?

@planettvguide is a TwitterBot that compares Wikipedia's list of American TV shows by date to a list of exoplanets with known distances from exoplanets.org to figure out when original broadcasts will reach exoplanets in real time. As it happens, they are posted to Twitter automatically.

How does it work?

  • Python
  • Tweepy for posting to Twitter
  • Some guts of OSCAAR for managing the exoplanets.org data
  • With a catalog of exoplanets from the great exoplanets.org
  • And this list of American TV shows by date from Wikipedia
