Research Mentorship: Pre-MAP (2014-2015)

In 2014 I joined the Astronomy Department's Pre-Major in Astronomy Program (Pre-MAP) as a research advisor to two undergraduate sophomore STEM majors: Ada Beale and Doug Branton. Together, we planned and proposed an observing campaign with the ARC 3.5 meter and ARCSAT 0.5 meter telescopes at Apache Point Observatory. So far we have been awarded two full nights in 2015 to observe newly classified metal-polluted, ZZ Ceti white dwarfs which may be good candidates for hosting planets or planetary debris. We will use high cadence photometry to study pulsations in the white dwarf atmospheres while searching for transiting planets.
