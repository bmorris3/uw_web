Highlights

Below are some of the places we expect you to visit within an hour's drive of Randazzo, our home base.

Randazzo

Small, medieval town high on Mt. Etna, built out of black stones from a lava flow. This is Rebecca's family's hometown. Join us here for a granita and brioche or a glass of sturdy local wine.

Taormina

A one hour drive from Randazzo, this tourist-friendly beach town is the best place on our list for shopping, ancient ruins, and getting a bite to eat using only English.

Castiglione di Sicilia

A 25 minute drive east from Randazzo, this mountain town is where the wedding venue is (venue name TBD).

Flights

Flights from New York to Catania are running around $1000 per person as of December 2015 (round trip). We suggest shopping around on CheapTickets or cheapOair for good rates.

Fly to:

Aeroporto Internazionale Catania (CTA), a one-hour drive from Randazzo.

Group flight?

Let us know if you're interested in booking a flight as a group from NY. We might be able to get a discount for a group fare if we can get a big enough group.

From the airport to Randazzo

Renting a car from the airport in Catania won't be absolutely necessary, as we'll try to coordinate as many rides as possible from family and friends in Randazzo. If you're arriving very early or late and soliciting rides isn't possible, you might take the bus.

Interbus from Catania to Randazzo via Taormina (one stop) for $15 (3 hours).

Rental cars: If you're looking for total independence, check here for rates for rental cars at the airport in Catania: Europcar, SIXT (starting around $100/day).
