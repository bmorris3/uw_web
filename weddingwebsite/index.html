Weather

The average high temperature at the time of the wedding should be near 82° F, with lows near 73°. During a typical August and September in Randazzo, it is only rainy for a combined total of four days, so the chance of rain during your stay is very small.

Highlights

Below are some of the places we expect you to visit within an hour's drive of Randazzo, our home base.

Randazzo

Small, medieval town high on Mt. Etna, built out of black stones from a lava flow. This is Rebecca's family's hometown. See our friend Marco's website on tourism in Randazzo for prettier pictures and things to do in town.

Taormina

A one hour drive from Randazzo, this tourist-friendly beach town is the best place on our list for shopping, ancient ruins, and getting a bite to eat using only English.

Castiglione di Sicilia

A 25 minute drive east from Randazzo, this mountain town is where the wedding venue is (venue name TBD).

Giardini Naxos

A one hour drive east from Randazzo, this is the nearest beach town for soaking up the sun on the Mediterranean. Pair a morning at Giardini with an evening in nearby Taormina.

Campagna (family vineyard)

In the fields just outside of Randazzo lies the small family olive grove and vineyard. Sophia will be hosting a small meal here for wedding attendees on the day after the wedding (Rebecca and Brett will be there too!).

Flights

Flights from New York to Catania are running around $1000 per person as of December 2015 (round trip). We suggest shopping around on CheapTickets or cheapOair for good rates.

Fly to:

Aeroporto Internazionale Catania (CTA), a one-hour drive from Randazzo.

Group flight?

Let us know if you're interested in booking a flight as a group from NY. We might be able to get a discount for a group fare if we can get a big enough group.

From the airport to Randazzo

Renting a car from the airport in Catania won't be absolutely necessary, as we'll try to coordinate as many rides as possible from family and friends in Randazzo.

If you're arriving very early or late and soliciting rides isn't possible, you might take the Interbus from Catania to Randazzo via Taormina (one stop) for $15 (3 hours).

Rental cars: If you're looking for total independence, check here for rates for rental cars at the airport in Catania: Europcar, SIXT (starting around $100/day).
